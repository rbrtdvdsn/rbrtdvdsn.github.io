Inspired by the fine folks at [Cortex] and their fondness for yearly themes—explained beautifully in [this video]—I’m embarking on the *Year of Growth* in 2022.

More specifically, I’m working to professionally expand beyond email and lifecycle marketing to become a more holistic growth marketer, cultivate stronger relationships with my family and friends, and generally mature as a person.

Rather than setting specific resolutions or goals right out of the gate, I’m working to the following ideal outcomes:
1. Cultivate a bias for action
2. Unlearn destructive behaviors
3. Build an additional revenue stream
4. Develop an at-home exercise routine
5. Explore my thoughts and feelings through writing
6. Discover a working schedule that allows me to flourish
7. Understand what helps me be the best version of myself
8. Define and document my personal & professional operating systems

In an attempt to monitor my progress toward the above outcomes, I’ll be asking these questions of myself each morning:
1. Was yesterday a good day?
2. Was I a good partner?
3. Was I a good friend?
4. Did I get 7+ hrs of sleep?
5. Did I exercise, beyond walking the dog?
6. Did I journal?
7. Did I read for pleasure?
8. Did I accomplish the day’s most important task?
9. Was I proactive or reactive?
10. Was I intentional with my time?
11. Did I pursue a new skill or interest?
12. Did I stick to my operating systems?

I’ll also be using this space to regularly document what I’m learning and interrogate my thoughts and feeling throughout the year.

So cheers to a year of self-exploration and personal growth! 🌱
